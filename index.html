Tilak Raj Chadha Institute of Management and Technology was built by Mukand Family to commemorate Prof. Tilak Raj Chadha, who became a living legend during his life-time. He was a veteran freedom fighter, prolific writer, fearless journalist, innovative educationist, selfless patriot, great humanist and above all a saint who relentlessly worked for the cause of morals and standards in education.
